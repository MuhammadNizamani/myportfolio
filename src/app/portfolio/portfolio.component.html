SMART AGRICULTURE

In agrarian Pakistan, farmers face challenges with soil fertility, characteristics, pH, and moisture. To address these issues, we propose a software-hardware solution that includes a cross-platform application built on "Flutter" using "Dart Programming Language" with algorithms, APIs, and backend techniques for soil analysis. The hardware component will utilize Arduino and moisture/pH sensors for detection. Computer vision will be employed to digitally investigate soil images and provide accurate results for soil pH. Our project aims to provide a combined software-hardware solution with computer vision for detecting soil moisture and pH levels accurately and equivalently.

Technologies: Flutter | Firebase | OpenCV | FastAPI | Arduino
